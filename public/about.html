About Us

“The Family at Red-Roofs” by Enid Blyton is a thrilling family adventure where Molly, Peter, Michael, and Shirley have their own adventure with their shares of ups and downs and happy endings. The hotel Red-Roof, established in 1995, got its name from this very book. This family-friendly hotel offers all the comforts you would need for an enjoyable holiday with your family. Rooms are equipped with all modern amenities like wifi, television, air conditioning, telephone, and a private bathroom with 24 hours hot water supply. The location of the hotel is very convenient for travelers coming by bus or train. The KTC bus stand is just a 5-minute walk and the Madgaon railway station is a 15 mins drive away. Goa is a land of sun and sand and a holiday at Goa is not complete without a visit to the beach. Hotel Red Roof is located close to the COLVA beach, MAJORDA beach, and BENAULIM beach. Family entertainment is also an essential part of a holiday. Ravindra-Bhavan the auditorium which is the center for all types of plays and theatricals is just a 10 min walk from the hotel. For movie lovers, there is an INOX which is again just a 5 min walk from the hotel. The Margao fish market and local market are also located very close to the hotel for tourists who love to explore the local, seasonal and traditional delicacies of Goa With Dominoes, KFC, and Pizza Hut nearby, you can get your fast food cravings satisfied in no time. Living at Red Roof means you're never far from a delicious meal. Come from Mumbai, Come from Pune, Come from any land, If you're coming to Goa, Stay at Hotel Red Roof to have a pleasant holiday.
